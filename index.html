BGA 200 Electric Leaf Blower

STIHL'S MOST POWERFUL BATTERY BLOWER

Description and features:

  • Quiet and pleasant operation for professional use in the city center and in noise sensitive environments.
  • Its innovative design is ideal for working in narrow and inaccessible spaces.
  • For large surfaces or long hours of work, the BGA 200 can be easily attached to the specially designed ErgoStand for increased comfort. You can switch between hand-carrying and cradle-carrying very quickly as needed. This makes the BGA 200 a 2-in-1 machine.
  • The curved design of the BGA 200 ensures that the blower does not bend or tilt despite strong airflow, thus relieving strain on the user's wrist.
  • The second handle and actuation lock provide additional comfort.
  • With the push of a button, the length of the blow tube can be easily adjusted to suit different cleaning situations, such as corner areas.
  • The comfortable standard ErgoBracket attaches to the hip harness of AR L backpack batteries
  • High-efficiency, virtually wear-free electric motor
